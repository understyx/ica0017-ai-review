6 ADVANTAGES OF CLOUD

§ Trade capital expense to variable expense
§ Benefit from massive economies of scale
§ Stop guessing about capacity
§ Increase speed and agility
§ Stop spending money on running and maintaining DC’s
§ Go global in minutes

1 – TRADE CAPEX FOR VARIABLE EXPENSE

Instead of having to invest a lot up to DC’s and
hardware before you know how you will end up using
them, you can pay for only when you use compute
resources and you pay for how much you consume

2 – BENEFIT FROM MASSIVE ECONOMIES OF SCALE

When was the last time Intel made a CPU specifically
for you?

3 – STOP GUESSING ABOUT CAPACITY

You do not know usually, where you end up, meaning
either buying too much or too little. If too much, then
you wasted resources or money, and if too little, you can
not offer competitive service. Cloud can scale with the
changing business needs

4 – INCREASE SPEED AND AGILITY

You can deploy your platform within hours or days, using
modern architecture principles. That scales infinitely with
demand.
Otherwise you must procure servers and rent DC space
and install everything

5 – STOP SPENDING MONEY RUNNING AND MAINTAINING DC’S

Focus on your core business or what you are good at,
not at managing facilities. Let someone else do that for
you.

6 – GO GLOBAL IN MINUTES

Easily deploy your application or service in multiple
locations around the world. This means low latency and
better experience for your customers at minimal cost
